Mission statement: To inspire and nurture the human spirit — one person, one candle and one neighborhood at a time. Our objective is to maintain "Arah's Candles" standing as one of the most recognized and respected brands in the world. To achieve this, we are continuing the disciplined expansion of our global store base, adding stores in both existing, developed markets such as the U.S., and in newer, higher growth markets such as the Philippines, as well as optimizing the mix of company-operated and licensed stores around the world. In addition, by leveraging the experience gained through our traditional store model, we continue to offer consumers new candles and other products in a variety of forms, across new categories, diverse channels and alternative store formats. We also believe our Arah's Candles Global Social Impact strategy, commitments related to ethically sourcing high-quality candles, contributing positively to the communities we do business in and being an employer of choice are contributors to our objective.
